About Event

​For those who love to sling code by day and grab a pint by night, get ready to have your minds blown (not just by the beer) at the grooviest taproom in town

​📅 When: July 8th
⏰ Time: 2 PM - 7 PM
📍 Where: Bira 91 Taproom, Kormangala, Bangalore

🎤 Agenda:

Learnings from attending 100+ open source meetups, events, & hackathons in the Bay Area
by Anshuman Pandey (Founder, NimbleBox.ai)

Desi Opensource Playbook: Learnings from OpenAssistant (35K GitHub stars) & BentoML (5K GitHub stars)
by Jithin "JJ" James (Ex-ML Engineer, BentoML) & Shahul ES (Contributor, OpenAssistant & Kaggle GM)

Building ChainFury: What I learnt from writing the first line of code to production
by Yash Bonde (Head of Research, NimbleBox.ai)

Overview of the LLM infra landscape
by Kaushik Bokka (Ex Sr Research Engg, Pytorch Lightning)

​Fireside chat: Taking ToolJet from 0 to 19K stars & over 365 contributors
with Navaneeth (Founder, ToolJet.com)

So, who should clear their calendar and grab a cold one with us?

  • ​Builders: Learn how to build great os projects. Real stuff that you can implement & use in your projects

  • ​Founders: Great for technical founders exploring & experimenting GenAI use cases at their startup

  • ​Investors: Exploring to invest in the future of infra, software & apps. Yes, AI will disrupt all sectors!

​💫 What's the Deal?

​We promise five hours of intellectually-stimulating, humor-infused talks from the Best of AI (and of course, a healthy supply of frothy, cold brews). And hey, if the talks don’t leave you speechless, the beer certainly will!
